6.2 Prototyping Summary

  • Successful proof of concept for the wheel-leg mechanism, validating the fundamental motion principles.
  • Functional hubless wheel with integrated wheel-leg structure.
  • Feasible and mechanically consistent linkage geometry.
  • Reliable initial mechanical motion performance.
  • Step-climbing and jumping motions partially validated through manual testing (control algorithm not yet implemented).

Overall, the prototype confirms the feasibility of the proposed design. While several refinements and minor redesigns are still required, the project will continue forward based on this validated configuration.
